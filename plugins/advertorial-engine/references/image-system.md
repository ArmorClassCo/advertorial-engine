# Image System

Every image slot in a copy doc carries seven fields (subject, composition,
style, aspect_ratio, placement_rationale, text_correlation, caption). The rules
below fill them non-arbitrarily.

## Cadence (how many images)

| archetype | images | cadence |
|---|---|---|
| offer-page hybrid / story advertorial | 25–45 slots | ~1 per 40–70 words |
| mechanism editorial | 20–40 | ~1 per 40–70 words |
| visual-panel advertorial | 30–45 | ~1 per 20–30 words (panels carry the copy) |
| numbered listicle | ~1 per item | + 2–4 in the offer zone |
| identity editorial | 15–25 | cards and tables, not photo walls |

Minimum: 12 slots on any advertorial. A page whose argument outruns its
evidence images reads as claims; the corpus's winners are image-saturated.

## Type mix & placement (position rules from the corpus)

- **Lifestyle/candid carries the narrative** (50–70% of images): age-matched
  subjects inside the trigger scenes (the stairs, the mirror, the 3 a.m.
  ceiling). UGC-grade candor outperforms studio gloss.
- **Before/after belongs to the proof zone**: mid-page on advertorials, in/after
  the offer zone on listicles. Ship as standardized card walls (photo pair +
  first name, age + two goal tags + one-line quote). Reuse the same wall as
  ambient proof if the page is long.
- **Product shots peak mid-to-late** (offer zones): in-hand scale shots,
  open-container texture proof, bundle flat-lays. Hero = product + evocative
  origin/context imagery.
- **Diagrams legitimize the mechanism** (mid-page): the named loop, ✗/✓
  comparison tables, multiplier tables, timelines. EVERY coined term gets a
  diagram.
- **Authority portraits sit at byline/expert positions** (early): headshot
  beside byline (listicles), professional-context portrait for expert personas.
  Real photography only for real people.

## The standardized proof units (compose these, not single images)

1. Before/after testimonial card (photo pair + name/age + goal tags + quote +
   product card + CTA under it)
2. Buy-box proof carousel: transformation collage → results timeline →
   success-rate graphic → return-policy graphic → ingredient/component list
3. Review-screenshot wall (platform-framed, name+age+date)
4. Component/provenance cards: origin label + stat chip each
5. Comparison-table image: product column vs the two alternatives THIS traffic
   is weighing
6. Stat graphic: decimal-precision percentage rendered as a chart

## Captions & the caption rule

The corpus uses NO figure captions (0 of ~2,000 content images). Caption work is
done by an adjacent one-line paragraph or baked into composite images. Default
every slot to `caption: none` and, where caption duty is needed, write the
one-liner as a body paragraph beside the slot.

## text_correlation — what an image is FOR

Every image lands the claim in its ADJACENT copy. Pair by type:
symptom scene ↔ trigger paragraph · before/after ↔ a specific
timeframe/result claim · diagram ↔ the named-mechanism sentence · multiplier
table ↔ a superiority claim · provenance card ↔ purity/origin claim ·
in-hand product ↔ usage-simplicity claim · authority portrait ↔ the credential
sentence · countdown/stock graphic ↔ the scarcity claim.
If a slot's text_correlation field can only say "sets the mood", cut the slot.

## Field-filling guide

- subject: a TERSE page-grade tag, ≤4 words, the way a live page labels its
  images — a noun label ("author photo", "results collage", "comparison
  table", "open jar texture") or an echo of the nearest heading. Put ALL
  art direction in composition/style — never in subject. Never use production
  vocabulary (no "carousel panel", "buy-box", "client-supplied", "hero asset")
  in any image field. Type keywords belong in the tag naturally (jar/pack →
  product; results/before-after → transformation proof; diagram/chart →
  mechanism; portrait/headshot → authority).
- composition: framing/shot AND the full scene brief ("close-up, eye-level:
  woman in her 50s pausing at a staircase landing, hand on rail")
- style: mood/finish ("candid smartphone realism, warm morning light" —
  UGC-grade for proof, editorial-clean for hero/identity)
- aspect_ratio: 4:3 or 1:1 in-body; 16:9 hero; 9:16 story-panel walls
- placement_rationale: why HERE in the flow ("lands after the failed-solutions
  purge to visualize the mechanism switch")
- text_correlation: the exact adjacent claim it substantiates
- caption: none (see caption rule) unless a one-line overlay is intrinsic
